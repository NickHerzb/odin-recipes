Sloppy Joes Recipe

Description:

desc

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 3/4 cup ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon yellow mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 6 hamburger buns, split

Steps:

  1. Cook the ground beef at medium heat in a skillet for 3 - 4 mins.
  2. Add the onion and the pepper and cook for another 3 - 5 mins.
  3. Stir in the rest of the ingredients and simmer on low for 20 - 30 mins.
  4. Divide onto buns.
