Pan Cubano Bread Recipe

Description:

This is a simple bread recipe to make some Pan Cubano bread.

Ingredients:

  • 480g bread flour
  • 260g water
  • 56g lard
  • 12g salt
  • 8g sugar
  • 7.5g instant yeast
  • 4g dried rosemary

Steps:

  1. Mix bread flour, lard, sugar, dried rosemary, and yeast in a bowl.
  2. Use a mixer with a bread hook to stir the dough as you add the salt and water in.
  3. Continue to mix for around 20 minutes.
  4. Cover the bowl and leave to rise for 1 hour.
  5. Take the dough out onto a non-stick sheet and press the dough into a 12" x 8" rectangle.
  6. Roll the dough up into a log and pinch the seam.
  7. Put the dough log onto a pan and cover in non-stick wrap.
  8. Leave the dough to rise again at room-temp for 30 minutes.
  9. Take the wrap off and put the dough into an oven set to 400F for 20 - 25 minutes.
  10. Take the bread out and leave it to cool for an hour.
